# Market Positioning & Differentiation

## Target Segments
1. **Tier 1: High-Volume Podcasters** (Need to turn 2-hour episodes into 20 clips).
2. **Tier 2: E-commerce Brands** (Need to turn product reviews into TikTok ads).
3. **Tier 3: Webinar & Workshop Hosts** (Need to extract "knowledge nuggets").

## SWOT Analysis
- **Strengths:** End-to-end automation, advanced NLP for captioning.
- **Weaknesses:** High GPU compute costs (Initial phase).
- **Opportunities:** Massive shift toward vertical video across all social platforms.
- **Threats:** Incumbent video editors (Adobe) adding AI features.

## Pricing Strategy
- **Freemium:** 3 clips per month (Watermarked).
- **Pro ($29/mo):** 30 clips, No watermark, Auto-captions.
- **Empire ($99/mo):** Unlimited clips, Viral Scoring, Priority GPU rendering.
